About Me

Hi. I'm Brent, and I use languages to pull nothing into somethingness.

Almost every action builds or demolishes, and there's no value in saying, "Look what I have destroyed."

It doesn't matter if I'm using words and sentences to create a story or variables and functions to compile an experience. At the end of the day, I want to be able to look at what I've accomplished and say, "Isn't that something?"
